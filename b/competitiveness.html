The Reality of a Saturated CS Job Market

Market Saturation:

•--As The Wall Street Journal highlights, the influx of CS graduates has created a saturated job market, reducing opportunities at top tech firms.

Tips to get and stay ahead

Redefining Career Paths:

•--Many graduates are finding opportunities outside traditional tech companies, applying their skills in non-tech fields where innovation is valued. This has been a great way for students to get their foot in the door and build up job experience.

A Student's Perspective:

•--Despite challenges, students like Het Sheth and Kanika Gupta from this article by penn state, believe the market saturation encourages creativity and opens doors for those willing to adapt.
